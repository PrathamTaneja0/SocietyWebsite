About VSIT

VSIT offers the students to opt for Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) and Master of Computer Application (MCA) programme. These programmes have been introduced to cater to the needs of a fairly large number of aspirants desirous of taking up Computer Application courses to secure entry into the fast expanding software and information processing world of the day. The course is fully equipped and rigorously conducted to provide sufficient background to graduates to work side by side with Computer Science Engineers and professionals in the Information Technology arena.

The emphasis is not only on acquiring theoretical knowledge but also on learning practical skills which are an indispensible part of the curriculum at VSIT. For the holistic development of our students, not only do we organize workshops, guest lectures and cultural events, but also encourage our students to improve upon their soft skills and life skills.
