Why Alankrit

Pursuing Art and Craft has the inherent benefits of encouraging the students to exercise flexible thinking, de-stress and explore their individual creativity. At VIPS-TC we encourage the students to display their artwork, motivating them to rejoice their hobby with increased vigour and enthusiasm.

About Us

Alankrit believes in rendering your thoughts in the form of art. We inspire the students to enhance their skills and to showcase their inspiring and beautiful art creations through various workshops, competitions and events. We celebrate every occasion terrifically, expressing our feelings in the form of art.

Our Work

We have organized "Artlytic" - an art and craft competition, Diya painting for a charity event on Diwali, "Kalakriti"-art competition portraying patriotism on Republic Day, World Earth Day etc.We are a group of aesthetes and connoisseurs that seeks/gives inspiration and ideas everywhere and anywhere.
